# AdBlock

Get warned about ads inside a level before playing!

## Features
- Shows a custom badge when browsing levels
- Shows the badge when going on the level
- Warning when playing the level first time
![AdBlock Badge](fullsound.adblock/resources/badge.png)


## Note
This mod is mostly a joke and a response to the ridiculous advertisement in *Skeletal Extremigans*. More levels may be added in future.
